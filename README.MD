# EV Market Analysis - Power BI Dashboard
A 5-page interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing pricing, performance, and sales across 20 global electric vehicle (EV) brands using a 2,000-row dataset.
---

## Dashboard Pages
| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Cover | Title page with interactive page navigation |
| Market Overview | KPI Cards, Brand distribution by country, market segment breakdown | 
| Pricing Analysis | Price vs range scatter plot, avg price by brand and variant |
| Performance & Specs | Horsepower vs acceleration, range, charging speed, battery capacity |
| Sales & Satisfaction | Sales volume, customer & safety ratings, brand leaderboard matrix |
---

## Key Insights
- **Tesla dominates global EV sales** with 101M+ annual units, followed by BYD and Volkswagen.
- ** Price does not drive range** - most brands cluster between 250-270 miles regardless of price
- **Honda is the hidden value leader** - lowest avg price ($56k) with the highest avg range (326 miles) among all brands
- **Porsche and Tesla lead DC fast charging** at 277–279 kW, a meaningful competitive differentiator
- **Lucid struggles despite premium pricing** — lowest customer rating (3.31) and among the lowest sales volume, despite a $192K avg price

## Dataset
- **Source:** [Kaggle — EV Market & Pricing Dataset 2026](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/patelris/electric-vehicle-market-and-pricing-dataset-2026)
- **Records:** 2,000 EV models
- **Features:** 25 columns including price, range, battery capacity, charging speed, horsepower, sales volume, customer rating, and safety rating
- **Brands:** 20 global brands across 6 countries (US, China, Germany, South Korea, Sweden, Japan)
